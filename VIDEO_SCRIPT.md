# VIDEO SCRIPT: Dawa_AI - Health at the Last Mile (3 Minutes)

**Visual:** Close-up of a dusty path in a rural village (e.g., Gishasha). A parent is looking worriedly at their sick child.

**Visual:** Screen recording of the terminal. We see the RAG system fetching a Malaria protocol and the AI generating a response in Kirundi.
